WHO WE ARE

 

We are a group of academics, researchers, editors. From assisting you with defining your study path, to research designs, training on statistical methods and applications of particular methodologies, we assist your degree path all along.

 

Despite our focus on MBA’s, we have a full range of services to students and potential students in a range of related disciplines, such as IT, economics, finance, behavioral subjects, and philosophy.

  • Research Design
  • Study Path
  • Methodologies
  • Statistics/Econometrics
  • Qualitative methods and tools
  • Editing and proofreading

OUR SERVICES

Our value proposition to you would depend on who you are and what stage of your educational career you are in. We treat each student individually with the goal of not only assisting with the completion of the degree but also with the aim of filling the gaps in the educational history and enabling the student to reach higher goals independently in the future.

RESEARCH DESIGN

Based on your goals, interests, and strengths, we assist with defining the research topic, assessing its viability, and planning its implementation.

STUDY PATH

Many of us struggled with the question: “Is this degree right for me? Is it worth time and money?”. At MBA Assist, we understand these questions and help you to design potential study and career paths that would suit your aspirations.

METHODOLOGIES

Many business students feel overwhelmed with the breadth of methodological choice for business studies. Our expert consultants will guide you through the intricacies and implications of different methodological choices.

STATISTICS/ECONOMETRICS

We understand that business students have great ideas and assist with mapping these ideas to the most suitable statistical methods for the implementation.

QUALITATIVE METHODS AND TOOLS

Our experts provide training on qualitative methods and assist with data preparation and handling.

EDITING/PROOFREADING

High-quality presentation is important for any work. We provide editing and proofreading services to bring your report to the executive-level quality.
